## 💡 The Genesis of This Project: A Tech Evolution
In 2023, I co-authored a scoping review published in the **Revista Brasileira de Educação Especial (RBEE)** evaluating the methodological quality of Animal-Assisted Interventions (AAI) across Latin America (available at: https://www.scielo.br/j/rbee/a/5zRCSqDvtW63wRJ8bbxdLKr/?lang=pt)[cite: 2]. Our analysis scrutinized 146 studies using the Cochrane Critical Appraisal Method and revealed a stark reality: **79.4% of the literature lacked quantitative statistical rigor, relying on small sample sizes and subjective, non-validated measurements[cite: 2].**

As a healthcare professional, I identified the gap[cite: 2]. **As a Healthcare Data Analyst, I built the solution.**

My current graduate studies in **Business Analytics** and technical training in **Python, SQL, and R** completely expanded my worldview. I realized that the lack of clinical evidence in rehabilitation isn't a medical limitation—it is a **data architecture problem**. This repository serves as a proof-of-concept pipeline demonstrating how modern data science techniques can solve the measurement bias and structural limitations highlighted in my previous academic work[cite: 2].

---

## 🛠️ Proposed Data Architecture & Methodology
This script (`clinical_aai_pipeline.py`) replaces paper-based clinical notes with a robust data pipeline model:

1. **EHR Data Modeling (SQL-like Structure):** Simulates a relational table structure that integrates patient demographics, diagnostic categories (Cerebral Palsy, ASD, Down Syndrome), and granular session tracking variables[cite: 2].
2. **Biomedical Telemetry Processing (Python Layer):** Simulates continuous longitudinal biomedical inputs (Heart Rate Variability - HRV) to shift the evaluation from qualitative observation to objective, sensor-driven physiological metrics[cite: 2].
3. **Biostatistical Validation (SciPy Inference):** Executes a Relational Paired t-test to calculate exact p-values, ensuring that therapeutic outcomes achieve undeniable mathematical significance, bypassing historical ambiguity[cite: 2].
